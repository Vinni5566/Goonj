## 💔 The Problem

138 million Indians over 60 carry decades of unwritten wisdom — folk recipes passed in whispers, dialect lullabies, prayers said at dusk, advice given between cricket commentary.

They share it every day. In voice notes, on WhatsApp.

And every single day, those voice notes vanish into chat history. A phone upgrade. A chat cleared. A memory, gone.

**1 in 3** elders live apart from their children. **20 million+** NRI families are separated by oceans. And there are **zero** apps built for this specific, heartbreaking gap.

Goonj was built to fill it.

---

## ✨ What Goonj Does

Goonj intercepts the voice notes your family already sends on WhatsApp — no new behavior, no new app, no learning curve for your dadi, nani, or amma — and turns them into a **searchable, living family memory vault**.

When your daughter asks *"how did dadi make kheer?"*, Goonj plays back **dadi's actual voice**, from the actual day she explained it.

Not a summary. Not a transcript. **Her voice.**

---

## 🔄 How It Works

Six steps. One forward. A lifetime.

| Step | Action | What Happens |
|------|--------|--------------|
| **1. Record** | Dadi sends a voice note on WhatsApp | Nothing changes for her |
| **2. Forward** | A family member forwards it to the Goonj number | One tap |
| **3. Process** | AI transcribes, tags language, identifies memory type | Invisible, instant |
| **4. Ask** | Grandson texts: *"how did dadi make that kheer?"* | Natural language query |
| **5. Listen** | Dadi's actual voice plays back — from the actual day | Not synthesis. The real thing. |
| **6. Remember** | A birthday memory surfaces automatically on the right day | Magic that feels earned |

```
You:    how did dadi make that kheer?
Goonj:  🎙️ Playing dadi's voice note from Jul 14, 2024
        ▏▎▍▎▌▍▎▌▏▎▍
```

## 🔒 Privacy & Ethics — Non-Negotiable

We built Goonj around a simple principle: *voices are sacred.*

- 🔐 **End-to-end encrypted** — Your family's voices are never exposed
- 🚫 **No voice synthesis** — We will never recreate or simulate a voice
- 🧠 **No training on family data** — Your memories don't train our models
- 🗑️ **Delete anytime** — Any family member can remove any memory, instantly
- 🌍 **Explicit vernacular consent** — Language settings are transparent and opt-in

We didn't build Goonj because it was a good market opportunity.
